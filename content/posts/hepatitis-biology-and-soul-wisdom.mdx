## The Medical Perspective: The Biological Guardian

From a scientific lens, **Hepatitis** refers to the inflammation of the liver. Its causes are diverse, ranging from viral infections (Hepatitis A, B, and C) and alcohol abuse to autoimmune disorders or toxic substances.

The liver is a vital engine that processes nutrients, detoxifies the blood, and manages metabolism. When this system is compromised, the body sends clear signals:
* **Extreme fatigue** and poor appetite.
* **Jaundice:** The yellowing of the skin or eyes.
* **Abdominal pain** and dark urine.

If left unaddressed, chronic cases can lead to liver scarring (cirrhosis), failure, or even cancer.

In energy medicine, the liver is often associated with anger, frustration, and repression. Author Caroline Myss describes the liver as the body's emotional filtration system, where unspoken rage, bitterness, or resentment may accumulate if not consciously released.

According to **German New Medicine**, liver inflammation is linked to a "fear of starvation" or survival conflicts, including financial insecurity or feeling emotionally unfed. Metaphysically, hepatitis may reflect:
1.  **Long-standing emotional toxicity:** Feeling "poisoned" by past experiences or relationships.
2.  **Self-directed anger:** Guilt that erodes our inner peace.
